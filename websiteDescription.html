Website Purpose

The Startup Checkup offers an easy way for stockholders of companies incorporated in Delaware to send their companies requests to inspect certain documents. Just provide us with the necessary information and follow the instructions, and you will have a letter that you can send to your company to make a formal request for inspection!

In other words, this website is the da bombdotcom.
