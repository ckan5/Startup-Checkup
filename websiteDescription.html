Website Purpose

The Startup Checkup offers an easy way for stockholders of Delaware-incorporated companies to send requests to inspect certain documents to their companies. This is statutorily allowed in the Delaware Code Title 8, section 220: Inspection of books and records. More information can be found here.

Just provide us with the necessary information, follow the instructions, and we will generate a letter that you can send to your company to make a formal request for inspection!

In other words, this website is da bombdotcom.
