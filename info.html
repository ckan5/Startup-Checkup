Are you the beneficial owner or the record owner of these shares?
A record owner of a stock is the person who is listed on the company's stockholder ledger as a stockholder.

A beneficial owner is someone who does not own the stock in their name but still receives a benefit from it. For example, you would be the beneficial owner of a stock if you controlled a legal entity that owned the shares in question.
